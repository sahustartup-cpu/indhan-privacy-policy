Privacy Policy for Indhan

Last Updated: May 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Indhan. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Indhan collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Indhan is a fuel information and nearby fuel pump discovery platform that helps users find nearby petrol pumps, check petrol and diesel prices, view fuel stock availability, and receive fuel-related information.

By using the Indhan application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

a) Location Information (GPS)

Indhan uses GPS and location services to provide nearby fuel pump discovery and location-based services.

We may collect:

  • Current device location
  • Nearby fuel station location data
  • Distance calculation information
  • Location-based recommendations

Location access may be used while the app is active to improve user experience and nearby fuel station accuracy.

b) Fuel Station & Petrol Pump Owner Data

Petrol pump owners, operators, or authorized partners may provide information through the Indhan platform including:

  • Petrol prices
  • Diesel prices
  • Fuel stock availability
  • Refuel and stock update information
  • Fuel station business details
  • Operational information related to pumps

This information helps users access updated fuel-related information nearby.

c) Device & Technical Information

We may automatically collect technical information including:

  • Device model and type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Crash reports
  • Performance analytics
  • Usage statistics

This data helps us improve app stability, performance, and user experience.

3. Fuel Prices & Stock Information Disclaimer

Fuel prices, fuel stock availability, refuel information, and petrol pump details displayed inside the Indhan application may be provided by petrol pump owners, business partners, public sources, third-party providers, or external services.

Indhan does not guarantee that all fuel prices, stock information, or availability data is fully accurate, complete, updated, or available in real-time.

Users should independently verify fuel prices, stock availability, and other important information directly with the petrol pump before making decisions.

4. How We Use Your Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Show nearby fuel pumps
  • Display petrol and diesel prices
  • Provide fuel stock information
  • Improve navigation and recommendations
  • Enhance app performance and stability
  • Improve user experience
  • Detect fraud or misuse
  • Provide customer support

5. Data Storage & Security

Indhan may securely store user and operational information using cloud-based or third-party storage systems.

We use reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

However, no online platform or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.
