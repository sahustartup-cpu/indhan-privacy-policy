Privacy Policy for Indhan

Last Updated: May 23, 2026

Welcome to Indhan. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Indhan collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the Indhan app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Location Information (GPS)

Indhan collects your device location using GPS services to provide features such as:

  • Nearby fuel pump discovery
  • Distance calculation to fuel stations
  • Better user experience and recommendations
  • Fuel availability and nearby pump tracking

Your location may be collected while using the app.

b) Fuel Station & Refuel Data

For petrol pump owners and authorized users, the app may collect:

  • Fuel stock updates
  • Petrol and diesel availability
  • Refuel activity information
  • Pump-related operational details

This information helps maintain accurate fuel availability data for users.

c) Device & Usage Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device type
  • Mobile operating system
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Crash logs
  • App usage analytics

This helps us improve app performance and reliability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Show nearby petrol pumps
  • Display fuel prices
  • Improve navigation and nearby services
  • Provide stock and refuel tracking
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, misuse, or unauthorized activity

3. Location Data Storage

Indhan may securely store location information to improve app functionality and provide a better experience for users.

We do not sell your personal location data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited information with:

  • Fuel station partners
  • Service providers that help operate the app
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell personal user information.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no internet-based service is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

Indhan may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Maps APIs
  • GPS/location services
  • Analytics tools
  • Cloud storage providers

These third-party services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

7. User Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction of data
  • Request deletion of data
  • Disable location permissions from device settings

8. Children’s Privacy

Indhan is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on our official website.
